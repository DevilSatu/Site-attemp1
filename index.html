The story of Fate/Zero begins with three mage families - Tohsaka, Einzbern, and Matou - joining forces to create the Holy Grail, which can grant any wish. However, they discovered that the Grail was cursed and became a source of evil. To purify the Grail, they created the Holy Grail War system, where seven mages (Masters) summon seven legendary heroes (Servants) to battle for the right to possess the Grail. In Fate/Zero, we witness the Fourth Holy Grail War, which takes place 10 years before the events of Fate/stay night. This war involves characters such as Emiya Kiritsugu, Kirei Kotomine, Tohsaka Tokiomi, and others, each with their own goals and desires. The war leads to tragic consequences, including the Fuyuki fire that claimed many lives. This event is directly connected to the beginning of the Fate/stay night story.
